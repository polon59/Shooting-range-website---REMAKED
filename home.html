Welcome to Shooting range!



The best equipped shooting range in Eastern Europe! If you are interested in firearms and you like shooting, or maybe you would like to try something new and exciting, then you're in the right place.

Shooting is a great way to spend free time, and for many people a hobby. We invite you to our shooting range.

Be sure to check our offer for individual clients, as well as for companies and institutions. We can provide transportation for groups. We also work with event agencies and we organize events with firearms. We have a great experience with tourist groups and stag party groups. Our arsenal and our instructors are waiting for you.
